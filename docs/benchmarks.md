## Methodology

### Data Sources
- **AIS Data**: Real-time vessel tracking from 105 commercial vessels
- **Weather Data**: NOAA/ECMWF weather forecasts and historical patterns
- **Fuel Data**: Industry-standard fuel consumption curves and chemistry models
- **Compliance Data**: IMO MEPC.245(66), MEPC.308(73), EU ETS regulations

### Validation Approach
1. **Baseline Calculation**: Historical fuel consumption without optimization
2. **Optimization Run**: Apply all 6 plugins with empirical fleet data
3. **Comparison**: Calculate fuel savings, CO‚ÇÇ reduction, and cost impact
4. **Verification**: Cross-validate against industry benchmarks (NAPA, DNV, ABB)
